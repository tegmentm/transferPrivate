Sabiha Gökçen Airport

Sabiha Gökçen Airport is one of the two airports in Istanbul. It is located on the Asian side of Istanbul.

If the passengers coming to Istanbul use Sabiha Gökçen Airport, they prefer private transportation services as well as public transportation to reach the city center or touristic areas.

The use of public transportation from Sabiha Gökçen airport to the city center, sultanahmet, and hotels in touristic areas is difficult as it is not usually possible to reach the destination with a single vehicle. Especially for people who do not have full command of the public transportation network of a huge metropolis like Istanbul, magic can lead to complexity.

Those looking for an alternative to public transportation consider airport taxis as the first alternative. However, as in many cities of the world, the taxi system in Istanbul is not suitable for comprehensive inspection. This is why many tourists are victimized by unqualified taxi drivers.
